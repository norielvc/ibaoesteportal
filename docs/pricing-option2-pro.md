The Barangay Digital Portal is an all-in-one web-based system that gives your barangay

The Pro Plan is designed for high-volume, urban, or growth-oriented barangays that need

## WHAT'S INCLUDED

### Public Landing Page (Your Barangay Website)
- Barangay name, logo, and custom color theme
- News & Events section with image carousel
- Barangay Officials directory with photos and positions
- Facilities section with image gallery and hero carousel
- Barangay Programs section
- Achievements & Awards section
- Contact Us section with hotlines
- Live clock display
- Custom domain setup (e.g., yourbrgname.gov.ph)
- Fully mobile responsive

### Certificate & Document Requests (All 11 Types)
- Barangay Clearance
- Certificate of Indigency
- Barangay Residency
- Business Permit (with Physical Inspection Report)
- Business Closure
- Co-habitation Certificate
- Medico-Legal Certificate
- Certification of Same Person
- Guardianship Certificate
- Natural Death Certificate
- Educational Assistance

### Workflow & Document Processing
- Full multi-step approval workflow
- Configurable steps per certificate type
- Assign specific staff to each approval step
- Actions: Approve, Reject, Return, Send Back
- Email notifications at every step (applicant + approvers)
- Official Receipt (OR) generation with PDF download
- Physical Inspection Report for Business Permits
- Pickup verification with digital signature capture
- Request history and full audit trail
- Resident database lookup
- Unlimited requests per month

### Staff & Admin
- Unlimited staff accounts
- Multiple admin roles
- Full admin dashboard
- Workflow configuration panel
- QR code employee attendance system (scan in/out)
- QR code generation per employee
- Analytics dashboard (requests by month, type, and status)
- Bulk data export (Excel / CSV)

### Support & Setup
- Full branding setup (logo, custom colors, barangay name)
- Custom domain registration and configuration
- Officials and staff data entry
- Staff account creation and orientation
- Data migration from old system (if applicable)
- 3 training calls (online or on-site)
- Priority email support (same-day response)
- Dedicated WhatsApp / Viber support line
- 30-day post-launch monitoring and support
